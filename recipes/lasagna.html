Lasagna

Description

Lasagne (Italian) is a type of pasta. It is usually served in overlapping sheets interspersed with layers of ingredients to taste, most often meat in bolognese sauce and béchamel sauce. Its origin is Italian and the dish usually prepared with meat is often called baked lasagne.

Ingredient

  • 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder Garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons MAGGI® Beef Bouillon
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1 sachet MAGGI® Tomato Bolognese Sauce Bolognese
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 3/4 pound of cooked lasagna pasta Precooked lasagna pasta
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a bowl, place the meat, garlic powder, MAGGI® Beef Bouillon 2, ground pepper and marinate for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large saucepan, place the oil, onion, garlic, celery and sauté for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the meat and cook for 10 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. Assembling the lasagne, when assembling the lasagne, grease an ovenproof dish or the tray you are using with a little oil.
    Pour a small amount of sauce, cover the bottom of the dish with slices of pre-cooked pasta, pour in a small amount of meat,
    add the mozzarella cheese and a small amount of Parmesan cheese.
  5. Repeat this procedure until the bowl is full. Finally, place a layer of pre-cooked pasta, the remaining sauce and cover with the cheeses.
    The lasagne must have a good amount of liquid for the pasta to cook. If it is too dry, chicken stock can be added.
  6. Cover with aluminium foil and bake in the oven at 150°C for 40 minutes.
  7. Additional step: Black olives and basil leaves can be added to this recipe when cooking the meat.
