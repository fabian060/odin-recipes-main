Venezuelan empanda

Description

An empanada or pie is a thin dough of bread, shortcrust pastry or puff pastry filled with a savoury or sweet preparation and baked or fried. The filling may include red or white meat, cheese, fish, vegetables or fruit.

Ingredient

  • 500 g wheat flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt

Steps

  1. Mix in a bowl the flour and water with a pinch of salt.
  2. let the dough rest for 5-10 minutes
  3. Heating the oil
  4. Roll out the dough into a circle.
  5. Add the desired filling in the middle of the rolled out dough.
  6. close the pie by joining the edges together
  7. and finally, fry in hot oil.
